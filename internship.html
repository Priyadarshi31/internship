COVID-19 DONATION

As the COVID-19 crisis continues to loom over us, The Government of jharkhand has taken a strict combat measure by directing a lockdown of the entire state. At this great hour of need, Our Foundation, in close coordination with State Governments & District Administration, has stepped in to provide relief by providing food to thousands of people across the country. By adhering to all safety and hygiene measures, our foundation has begun its relief service by providing meal or packed grocery kits to the marginalised and low-income segment of the society comprising of daily wage workers, migrant labourers, construction site workers, and needy people at old age homes and night shelters. Presently, the Foundation and its Association Foundations are providing food relief in many different districts.
